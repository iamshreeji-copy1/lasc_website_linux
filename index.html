About Us

Welcome to SATs (Semiconductor Analysis and Testing Solutions), where innovation meets precision in the semiconductor industry. Our company is driven by a team of active researchers who have been deeply engaged in state-of-the-art research in the semiconductor domain for over 8 years. We are dedicated to advancing technology and providing cutting-edge solutions for semiconductor analysis and testing.

Vision

Our vision is to promote the "Make in India" initiative by developing and manufacturing automatic precision scientific instruments for semiconductor testing in research and development. We aim to position India as a leader in semiconductor technology through our innovative solutions.

Mission

  • Promote "Make In India"
  • Promote "Green Energy Solutions"
  • Promote "Research & Development through specialized instruments"

Our mission is to forecast the future of green energy, particularly solar energy, and support its advancement through our specialized instruments and services. We are committed to contributing to a sustainable future by providing the tools necessary for groundbreaking research and development in the semiconductor industry.

Our Services

At SATs, we are committed to providing comprehensive and innovative solutions for semiconductor analysis and testing. Our range of services and products is designed to meet the diverse needs of researchers and developers in the semiconductor industry. Here’s what we offer:

Micro Probe Stations

Our high-precision micro probe stations are ideal for detailed semiconductor testing and analysis. These stations provide accuracy and reliability, essential for advanced research.

Sample Holders

We offer a variety of sample holders that are designed to securely hold different types of semiconductor samples, ensuring stability and precision during testing procedures.

Read more

Autoclaves

Our autoclaves are equipped with advanced features to provide efficient and effective sterilization for your semiconductor equipment and samples.

Read more

PEC Setups

We provide Photoelectrochemical (PEC) setups that are essential for studying the interactions between light and semiconductor materials, facilitating cutting-edge research in solar energy and other applications.
